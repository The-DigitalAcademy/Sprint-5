About Us

DISCOVER YOUR POTENTIAL!


From our industrial birthplace in Braamfontein, Gym Club has grown to become Joburg's premier fitness community.
With state-of-the-art equipment and fitness classes in an inclusive atmosphere,
Gym Club is like no other gym outside of Johannesburg.
Our new site in the heart of Braamfontein takes our ethos up a notch.
We’ve combined the elements that make us so great with our renewed commitment to advancing your health and wellbeing.
We’ve invested in some of the world’s best fitness equipment to optimise your workouts.
Our various exercise classes are designed to empower you through the many benefits of individual/group fitness.

At our core, we’re a strength and conditioning gym but we recognise the latest fitness trends.
We’ve tapped into the increasingly-popular yoga, spin and HIIT markets to bring fitness fans of all abilities the workout they crave.

Gym Club is premium fitness with a focus on inclusivity and positivity.
It’s important to us that our gyms are cutting-edge but also welcoming atmosphere ,
so everyone can achieve their fitness goals. We rise by lifting others. Together,
we can help you rise above everything to become the best version of you.
